Frequently Asked Questions

Q: My recommendations are telling me that I am consuming more than twice the recommended daily amount!  Is something wrong?

A: You might be submitting your weight in kilograms!  FFP is designed for use with imperial weight units.  Please round your weight to the nearest pound.

Q: How accurate are FFP’s suggestions?

A: Although accurate, FFP suggestions are not a substitution for recommendations from a personal physician or dietitian.  FFP’s generalized recommendations based on demographic data from the Mayo clinic

Q: Is FFP a free service?

A:  Yes! All features are available for free for all users.

Q: Where do the recommended numbers come from?

A:  Using the height and weight you input on the site, we calculate based on equations from some of the most trusted medical institutes such as the Mayo Clinic.

Q: Do these plans account for allergies?

A: We do account for allergies in our plan as each one is tailored for a specific person.
